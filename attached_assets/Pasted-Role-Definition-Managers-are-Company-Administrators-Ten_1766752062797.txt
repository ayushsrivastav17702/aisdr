Role Definition
Managers are Company Administrators (Tenant Admins) created by the Super Admin when their company signs up for AiSDR. They have full control over their company's AiSDR instance, including creating and managing Users (SDRs/BDRs), campaigns, and all settings within their tenant. They bridge strategy and execution, managing multiple team members and campaigns while optimizing for team and organizational goals.
Hierarchy Position
Super Admin (AiSDR Platform Owner)
    ↓ Creates tenant and Manager account
Manager (Company Administrator) ← YOU ARE HERE
    ↓ Creates and manages
Users (SDRs/BDRs in your company)
How You Got This Account

Your company signed up for AiSDR
The Super Admin (AiSDR team) created your company's tenant/account
The Super Admin created your Manager login credentials
You received a welcome email with login details
You are the administrator for YOUR company's instance only
You cannot see or access other companies' data

Key Responsibilities

Team performance management
Campaign strategy and oversight
Pipeline and forecast management
Resource allocation (mailboxes, budgets)
Quality control and compliance
Reporting to leadership
Team coaching and development
Cross-functional collaboration

Success Metrics

Team meetings booked per month

Team quota attainment
Campaign ROI
Team productivity (campaigns launched, prospects contacted)

3. Manager User Workflows
3.1 Daily Manager Workflow
Morning Routine (15-20 minutes):

Check team dashboard for overnight activity
Review meetings booked yesterday
Check for any urgent alerts (compliance, performance)
Review campaigns pending approval
Quick scan of reply inbox for escalations
Check team member activity levels

Mid-Day Check (10 minutes):

Monitor today's send volume and pacing
Check reply rates and responses
Review any new escalated conversations
Approve/reject pending campaigns
Address team questions or blockers

End-of-Day Review (15 minutes):

Review daily performance vs. goals
Identify top and bottom performers
Plan next-day priorities
Schedule 1:1s if needed
Prepare for tomorrow's campaigns
Update stakeholders on progress

3.2 Weekly Manager Workflow
Monday: Planning & Goal Setting

Review last week's performance
Set this week's priorities
Assign new prospect lists
Launch new campaigns
Team standup meeting

Mid-Week: Performance Management

Campaign performance reviews
Coach underperformers
Optimize struggling campaigns
Resource reallocation if needed
Quality spot checks

Friday: Analysis & Reporting

Generate weekly team report
Analyze trends and patterns
Document wins and learnings
Plan next week's strategy
Share updates with leadership

3.3 Monthly Manager Workflow
Week 1: Month Kickoff

Review last month's results
Set monthly goals and quotas
Plan major campaigns for the month
Resource allocation
Team training session

Week 2-3: Execution & Optimization

Monitor progress to monthly goals
Mid-month forecast update
Campaign optimization
Address performance issues
1:1s with each team member

Week 4: Month Close

Final push to hit goals
Generate monthly reports
Analyze what worked/didn't work
Plan improvements for next month
Celebrate wins with team
Submit reports to leadership

3.4 Campaign Launch Approval Workflow

Receive Notification:

Team member submits campaign for approval
Notification in platform and email


Initial Review:

Check campaign objectives
Review ICP and targeting criteria
Validate prospect count and source
Check resource allocation (mailboxes)


Message Quality Review:

Read through sequence messages
Check tone and brand alignment
Verify personalization quality
Review subject lines
Test for spam triggers


Compliance Check:

Unsubscribe link present
Company information in footer
No prohibited content
Send limits appropriate
Approved domain usage


Decision:

Approve: Campaign launches immediately or scheduled
Request Changes: Send back with specific feedback
Reject: Explain reasoning and provide guidance


Monitor Post-Launch:

Check first 24-hour performance
Verify deliverability
Monitor early replies
Intervene if issues arise

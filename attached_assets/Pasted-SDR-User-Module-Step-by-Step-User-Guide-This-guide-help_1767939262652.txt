SDR User Module – Step-by-Step User Guide

This guide helps a new SDR (User) go from first login to booking meetings, covering every module and step in the correct order. Follow this sequentially for best results.

1. First Login & Account Setup
Step 1.1: Login

Use credentials shared by your Manager

On first login, you’ll be prompted to update your password

Step 1.2: Complete Your Profile

Navigate to Profile Settings

Update:

Name & role

Timezone (critical for send timing)

Preferred language (if applicable)

✅ Outcome: Your account is active and personalized

2. Connect Your Mailbox
Step 2.1: Add Mailbox

Go to Mailbox Settings

Click Connect Mailbox

Choose provider (Google / Outlook)

Complete OAuth authentication

Step 2.2: Verify Mailbox Health

Ensure status = Connected & Verified

Send a test email if available

⚠️ Sequences and campaigns will NOT run without a connected mailbox

3. Understand the 9-Stage Workflow

Your account progresses through mandatory stages:

Profile Ready

Mailbox Connected

Prospect Added

Sequence Created

Personalization Configured

Delay Rules Set

Campaign Activated

Emails Sent

Analytics Available

Each stage must be completed to move forward.

4. Prospect Discovery & Import
Option A: AI Prospect Search

Go to Prospects → AI Search

Enter a natural language query

Example: “US SaaS companies, 50–200 employees, Head of Sales”

Review parsed filters

Run search

Option B: Import Prospects

Upload CSV or add manually

Required fields:

Email

First name

Company name

Step 4.3: Data Enrichment

System auto-enriches using:

LinkedIn

Company data

News & intent signals

✅ Outcome: Prospects are enriched and ready

5. Create a Sequence
Step 5.1: Create New Sequence

Go to Sequences → Create New

Name your sequence clearly

Example: “US SaaS – Cold Outreach – Q1”

Step 5.2: Add Steps

Supported step types:

Email

Wait / Delay

Example structure:

Email 1

Wait 2 days

Email 2

Wait 3 days

Email 3

6. Configure AI Personalization
Step 6.1: Enable AI Personalization

Toggle AI Personalization ON

Step 6.2: Select Personalization Inputs

AI can use:

LinkedIn headline & recent activity

Company description

Recent company news

Job role & seniority

Step 6.3: Preview Personalization

Preview samples for 5–10 prospects

Lock content once satisfied

⚠️ Always review previews to avoid hallucinations

7. Set Delay & Send Rules
Step 7.1: Delay Configuration

Define wait time between steps (hours or days)

Step 7.2: Send Window

Configure allowed send days

Configure send hours (local timezone)

8. Add Prospects to Sequence
Step 8.1: Select Prospects

Filter prospects

Select individually or bulk select

Step 8.2: Enroll in Sequence

Click Add to Sequence

Confirm enrollment

System validates:

Email limits

Active enrollments

Campaign quotas

9. Activate Campaign
Step 9.1: Final Checklist

Ensure:

Mailbox connected

Sequence active

Personalization locked

Delays configured

Step 9.2: Activate

Click Activate Campaign

Campaign status changes to Running

10. Monitor Email Sending
What Happens Automatically

Emails are sent one-by-one

Delays respected

Provider delivery acknowledged

Expected Timelines

Personalization: seconds per prospect

Email send: queued → provider → sent

11. Reply Management (Inbox)
Step 11.1: View Replies

Go to Inbox

Replies appear in the same thread

Step 11.2: AI Reply Assistance (If Enabled)

AI suggests reply drafts

You can edit or send manually

Step 11.3: Manual Actions

Reply

Pause prospect

Mark as Interested / Not Interested

12. Analytics & Performance Tracking
What You Can Track

Emails sent

Open & reply rates

Campaign performance

Sequence effectiveness

Use Analytics To

Improve copy

Adjust delays

Stop underperforming sequences

13. Quotas & Limits

You are limited by:

Emails per day

Active enrollments

Active campaigns

Always check quota before launching new campaigns.

14. Common Mistakes to Avoid

Activating campaigns without previews

Adding too many prospects at once

Ignoring reply handling

Overlapping sequences

15. Daily SDR Workflow (Recommended)

Check Inbox replies

Review campaign health

Add new prospects

Launch or optimize sequences

Review analytics

Final Note

This system is designed to enforce best practices. If something is blocked, the platform will tell you what and why. Follow the workflow and you’ll avoid most issues.

❗ D. Adversarial Tests (HIGHLY recommended for AI SDR)

This is where AI fails the most.

Test prompts like:

Very vague ICP: “Find companies doing something with clouds”

Toxic or unsafe inputs

Junk inputs: “asdfasdf generate email pls”

Extremely long inputs (10k tokens)

Conflicting instructions

100 rapid requests (spam/flooding)

Goal: prevent broken emails, hallucinations, and bad enrichment.
